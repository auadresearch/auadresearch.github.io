Active Directory Security Research Group

Welcome to the Active Directory (AD) Security Research Group at the Adelaide University. Our group, led by Prof. Hung Nguyen, is dedicated to addressing the most critical security challenges in enterprise-level AD. We develop novel models, algorithms, dataset, and metrics to help organizations understand, prioritize, and remediate complex vulnerabilities in the world's most widely used identity management system.

Our Research Focus

Our work spans several key areas of Active Directory security, aiming to create solutions that are not only theoretically sound but also practical for real-world application. This research includes developing methods for effective vulnerability prioritization to help organizations focus on the most critical threats. To enable robust and repeatable experiments, we also build tools for synthetic data generation. Additionally, we design novel security metrics, such as 'choke-point' analysis, to provide a deeper and more accurate understanding of network risk.
